  • About Linksxup

Linksxup is an online community forum, micro blogging and media sharing site for stakeholders of the University of Benin (UNIBEN). Linksxupers(users) can share content and freely express their thoughts or feelings by posting moments, stories, text, links, images, audios, documents and videos. Users can also make remarks on linkupdates/post to provide discussions and group or personal amusement.

  • Vision

We seek to link up uniben stakeholders with one another so they can instantly and convenietly search for, support, create and share ideas, knowledge, information and unique experiences with one another.

  • Mission

Linksxup's mission is to connect and bring together aspirants, students, alumni, academic, non-academic staff and other stakeholders of UNIBEN as well as to build a productive community which will have meaningful discussions on topics of interest by:
  • Sharing breaking news and information to enable everyone stay updated/informed(Know whatsup).

  • Building an effective and productive mentorship system.

  • Communicating your ideas or asking questions and getting helpful feedbacks.

  • Freely express your thoughts, ideas and feelings.

  • Learning from each other's shared experiences.

  • Build a fascinating profile
    Customise your profile, add a profile photo, discriptive bio, profession/skills, location

  • Discussing topics of interest and experiences with linksxupers (users) through linkupdates, remarks and replies
    Staying abreast with trends via your linkups notifications and updates.

  • Easily find, create and categorise content using (#)
    A variety of predefined (#) tags are made available for users to categorise their content under topics such as: education, career, music, movies, fashion, sports, games, food, relationship, general information and lots more... You can also create your own unique (#) tags.
